Post-Millennial Pop Music Corpus Analysis

  • Collaborated with UMass's Music Department on corpus-based research of the top 50 songs of the past 20 years.
  • Analyzed corpus using k-means clustering in R to group songs together based on rhythmic similarity.
  • Developed Python scripts and tools to automate portions of the song annotation process for current and future research participants.
  • Consolidated old data sets and standardized annotation formats of over 70 songs to improve further analysis of the corpus.

chordHMM

  • Collaborated with UMass Music Professor Chris White to update an old hidden markov model (HMM) from Python 2 to Python 3.
  • chordHMM trains and tests on a corpus of chords, and then spits out the best number of HMM hidden states with transition and emission probabilities.
  • Integrated the Python script into a Google Colab.
